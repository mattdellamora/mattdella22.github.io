Museo Vincenzo Vela
SUPSI, 2019

The Vincenzo Vela museum in Ligornetto asked to think of a platform that could archive all the information about the sculptures of Vincenzo Vela spread all around the world. I produced images, animations and prototypes to explain the concept of this physical installation which had to be placed inside of the building's space but also be flexible enough to be used on smaller device. My project revolved around the idea of the distances of the sculptures from Ligornetto, pivotal origin of Vincenzo Vela's work.

User Interface, User Experience, web development

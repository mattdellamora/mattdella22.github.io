Luoghi e voci
Fondazione Monte Verità, 2021

Monte Verità has always been a magnetic pole of convergence of ideas, trends, experiments and historical figures, and is still today one of the most interesting cultural centres of the region. During this year's edition of "Giardini in arte", the exhibition "Luoghi e voci" took place: in 2020 a group of four artists undertook a residence during which they soaked up the spirit of the place and engaged in a dialogque with nature, the buildings and symbolic places. The exhibition features thirteen works located all over the hill and its iconic landmarks, like the Bauhaus–style hotel and the light-and-air huts. This A3 foldable flyer I produced, tells the event and the individual concept of the four artist, and invites the user to discover the exhibition.

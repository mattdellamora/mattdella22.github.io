Award Publication 2023
Junge Grafik, 2023

Last November, the 2025 Junge Grafik Award ceremony united this year’s champions. In close collaboration, Anna Tuena and I designed the publication launched at the Award party.

Inspired by a sticker album, the concept presents the winners as true champions, placing each project in the spotlight and highlighting its unique features. The first section functions as a collectible album, inviting readers to stick in projects one by one. It is followed by two sports newspaper–inspired sections with reports, stats and image spreads celebrating the third edition.

Design: Mattia Dellamora, Anna Tuena
Text: Mattia Dellamora, Anna Tuena, Arta Shala

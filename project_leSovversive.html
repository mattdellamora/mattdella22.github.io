Le Sovversive
Festival's Visual Identity

Le Sovversive is a transdisciplinary and intersectional festival dedicated to bottom-up movements. The first edition in 2025 focused on exploring gender and sexuality as political, affective, and transformative territories. Communication took place in form of a flyer and social media content. A system was designed to accommodate different types of content through the use of frames and colors. In the absence of visual materials to promote individual events or information, the same visual system was used to create illustrations or motion graphics.

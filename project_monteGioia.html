Monte Gioia
Editorial Design

In collaboration with Nadine Curanz, I designed this immersive guide for artists Dorothé Thébert and Filippo Fillinger, for the project they realized during their residency at Monte Verità.

The outcome of their residency took the form of a literary walk. A path with 12 moments, where various characters read aloud texts written by the artists. The publication, conceived as both a guide and a companion to the experience, invites readers to engage with the journey in a truly immersive way.
